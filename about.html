Bio

Alexander Gumuchian — known as bbno$ (short for "baby no money") — grew up in Vancouver and got into music through self-teaching and experimenting with production software. He briefly attended UBC before dropping out to go all-in on music.


He started posting on SoundCloud in 2017 and built a following through his weird, catchy, self-deprecating style. Everything exploded in 2019 when Lalala featuring Y2K went viral on TikTok.


"I just wanted to make the weirdest, funniest, most fun music I could."

He has stayed fully independent his whole career — no major label deals. He handles his own releases and tour, which is a big reason his fans trust him so much.
